Help & FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I update or delete an item?

A: To update or delete, you must type the exact Item Name in the Manage page form. The system uses the name as the key to find the item.

Q: Why did my data disappear?

A: This application is a standalone frontend system. The data is saved in your browser's current session. If you refresh the page or close the browser, the data will be reset.

Troubleshooting Guidance

  • Cannot click the "Add Item" button?
    Check if you have filled out all the required fields (ID, Name, Supplier, Quantity, Price). The button remains disabled until the form is valid.
  • Error saying "Numbers cannot be negative"?
    Ensure that the Quantity and Price fields do not have a minus sign.
  • Item not found when searching?
    Check for typos. The search function is case-insensitive, but you must spell the word correctly.
